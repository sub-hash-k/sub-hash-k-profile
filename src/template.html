Project Higlights

Mergers and Aquisitions

Actuarial support for the IPO preparation work of ICICI Prudential and HDFC Life. Developed cash flow models in MS Excel for non-participating, participating, and unit-linked business to review client's Prophet model. One of the challenge was to execute millions of policy records through MS Excel - developed a solution to spread the calculations over multiple cores of a machine to achieve faster model runtime and to automate the results collation and checking. Reviewed the components of embedded value, analysis of movement, value of new business, and sensitivity results.

Multiple M&As targeting life insurance companies in India, Asia, and the Middle East. Developed aggregate models to calculate appraisal value and financial planning models to project the target's financial statements. Performed due diligence to identify risks in the target's business.

Led the transition of the actuarial function to Milliman. Currently, responsible for Prophet model maintenance, quarterly valuations, and solvency assessment across the following reporting basis: Hong Kong, Singapore RBC2, IFRS, UAE RBC, and the Isle of Man RBC (similar to Solvency II).


Financial reporting - US GAAP and IFRS 4
