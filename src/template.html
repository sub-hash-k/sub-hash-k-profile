Project Higlights

Valuation and reporting

I have supported multiple M&As and embedded valuations of life insurance companies in India. Besides my experience with Indian insurance companies, I gained limited exposure to Takaful insurance on one of my M&A project involving a Takaful family business.

I have extensive experience working with statuary reserving. My projects include:

a) Quarterly and yearly statutory reporting for a life insurer with branches in Hong Kong, Singapore, UAE, and Isle of Man;

b) Quarterly statutorty valuation for a client in Singapore; and

c) IFRS 4 reporting for fixed deferred annuity line of business for a large insurer in the US.

Insurance products design and pricing

Reviewed multiple product applications and commented on product design and features to support a regulator in the Middle East. I have worked on the pricing / profit testing of the following products:

a) unit linked insurance b) term insurance c) deferred annuities

Cash flow modelling

I have many years of experience developing actuarial models and I have developed a knack for the modelling best practices. Some of my work includes Prophet modelling for risk based capital calculations (RBC2 implementation for clients in Singapore), asset-liability modelling, Prophet modelling for IFRS 17 implementation, designing and implementing actuarial model from scratch for a new life insurer in Singapore, multiple model enhancemnts and reviews for clients in India.

I am extremely good at managing compex modelling projects and I have experience of working on the following actuarial systems: Prophet, Prophet Enterprise, MoSes, Risk Agility and MS-Excel (despite all the limitations, I love cash flow modelling using MS Excel).

Tools and utilities

I am learning to code and have built the following tools to support my work: a) web analytics dashboard using Adobe Flex; b) parallel processing on MS Excel for aggregate portfolio runs of cash flow models; c) power BI dashboard to track utilization; d) python scripts to automate work flow; etc.
